Funding type
Merchant
Advance Funding
  • A merchant advance is a form of business financing in which a lump-sum payment is given to a business in exchange for an agreed-upon portion of future sales.

    • This type of financing normally has shorter payment terms and smaller regular payment amounts.

    It’s a suitable solution for a wide range of businesses with a high volume of credit card transactions, such as restaurants, medical practices, retailers and more.
